INDIVIDUAL SIDE LETTER

to Employment Agreement EA-2023-077 — Remote Work Arrangement

Date: 20 February 2025

Parties: Helios Digital GmbH ("Employer") and Mert Yilmaz ("Employee")

1. Background

1.1 The Employer has issued an updated company-wide remote work policy dated 5 February 2025. That policy expressly permits individually agreed exceptions in writing.

2. Individual Arrangement

2.1 In recognition of the Employee's role and performance, the parties agree that the Employee may work remotely three (3) days per week.

2.2 This individual arrangement prevails over the general company remote work policy and over Section 4.1 of the Employment Agreement.

3. Duration

3.1 This arrangement remains in effect for as long as the Employee holds the role of Senior Software Developer, unless replaced by a later written agreement.
