SUBJECT: Updated Company-Wide Remote Work Policy (effective 1 March 2025)

Dear colleagues,

As part of our return-to-office initiative, the company is updating its
remote work policy.

  NEW POLICY (effective 1 March 2025):
  - Remote work is reduced to a MAXIMUM OF ONE (1) DAY PER WEEK for all
    employees.
  - This replaces any previous standard of two days per week.
  - This general policy overrides individual employment contracts UNLESS
    a separate individual arrangement has been agreed in writing with an
    employee.

Team lead approval is required for the chosen remote day.

Thank you for your cooperation,
