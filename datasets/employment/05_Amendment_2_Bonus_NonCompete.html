AMENDMENT No. 2

to Employment Agreement EA-2023-077 — Bonus, Notice & Non-Compete

Effective Date: 1 January 2026

Parties: Helios Digital GmbH ("Employer") and Mert Yilmaz ("Employee")

1. Bonus

1.1 The bonus is changed from discretionary to a guaranteed annual bonus of 12% of the gross annual salary, payable each March for the preceding year.

2. Notice Period

2.1 The notice period for termination by either party is amended to three (3) months to the end of a calendar month.

3. Post-Contractual Non-Compete

3.1 Section 7.1 of the Employment Agreement is amended so that the post-contractual non-compete period is reduced from twelve (12) months to six (6) months.

4. No Other Changes

4.1 All other terms (as amended), including salary, leave and the individual remote work arrangement of 20 February 2025, remain in full force and effect.
