AMENDMENT No. 2

to Commercial Lease CL-2024-118 — Parking Charges

Effective Date: 1 September 2025

Parties: Rheinland Property Holding GmbH ("Landlord") and Nimbus Software GmbH ("Tenant")

1. Purpose

Following refurbishment of the building garage, the parties agree to revise the parking arrangements previously set out in the Lease and the Side Letter dated 15 February 2024.

2. Parking Charge

2.1 Effective 1 September 2025, parking is no longer free of charge. The Tenant shall pay EUR 80.00 per space per month.

2.2 The Tenant retains its eight (8) parking spaces as granted by the Side Letter. Accordingly, the additional monthly parking charge is 8 × EUR 80.00 = EUR 640.00 per month.

3. Interaction with Side Letter

3.1 This Amendment modifies only the cost of parking. The Tenant's entitlement to eight (8) spaces under the Side Letter remains unchanged. To the extent the Side Letter states that parking is provided "at no additional charge", that wording is superseded by this Amendment.

4. No Other Changes

4.1 All other terms of the Lease (as amended) remain in full force and effect.
