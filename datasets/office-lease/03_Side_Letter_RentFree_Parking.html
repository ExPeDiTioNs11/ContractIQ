SIDE LETTER

to Commercial Lease CL-2024-118 — Incentives & Parking

Date: 15 February 2024 (executed together with the Lease)

Parties: Rheinland Property Holding GmbH ("Landlord") and Nimbus Software GmbH ("Tenant")

1. Rent-Free Period

1.1 As a leasing incentive, the Tenant shall enjoy a rent-free period for the first three (3) months of the term (March, April and May 2024). Service charges remain payable during this period.

2. Parking

2.1 Notwithstanding Section 6.1 of the Lease, the Tenant shall be entitled to eight (8) parking spaces (not five), at no additional charge.

3. Precedence

3.1 In the event of any conflict between this Side Letter and the Lease, the terms of this Side Letter shall prevail.

This Side Letter forms an integral part of the lease arrangement between the parties and is signed contemporaneously with Lease CL-2024-118.
