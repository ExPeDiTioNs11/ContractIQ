COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

Lease No. CL-2024-118 — Office Premises

Effective Date: 1 March 2024

Landlord: Rheinland Property Holding GmbH, Mediapark 8, 50670 Cologne

Tenant: Nimbus Software GmbH, Cologne

1. Premises

1.1 The Landlord lets to the Tenant the office unit on the 3rd floor of Mediapark 8, 50670 Cologne, with a lettable area of 420 m² (the "Premises").

2. Rent & Service Charges

ItemAmount
Base rentEUR 12,600.00 per month (EUR 30.00 / m²)
Service charge (Nebenkosten)EUR 1,890.00 per month (EUR 4.50 / m²)
PaymentMonthly in advance, due by the 3rd working day

3. Term & Renewal

3.1 The lease runs for a fixed term of five (5) years, from 1 March 2024 to 28 February 2029.

3.2 The Tenant has a one-time option to extend the lease by a further five (5) years, exercisable by written notice no later than twelve (12) months before expiry of the initial term.

4. Rent Review (Indexation)

4.1 The base rent shall be adjusted annually on 1 January in line with the German Consumer Price Index (CPI), based on the change over the preceding twelve months.

5. Security Deposit

5.1 The Tenant shall provide a security deposit equal to three (3) months' base rent, i.e. EUR 37,800.00, held by the Landlord for the duration of the lease.

6. Parking

6.1 The Tenant is entitled to five (5) parking spaces in the building garage at no additional charge for the duration of the lease.

7. Subletting

7.1 The Tenant shall not sublet the Premises or any part thereof, nor assign this lease, without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

8. Maintenance

8.1 The Tenant is responsible for the interior maintenance of the Premises. The Landlord is responsible for the structure, roof, and common areas.

9. Governing Law

9.1 This lease is governed by German law. Place of jurisdiction is Cologne.
