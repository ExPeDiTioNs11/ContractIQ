AMENDMENT No. 1

to Commercial Lease CL-2024-118 — Annual Rent Indexation

Effective Date: 1 January 2025

Parties: Rheinland Property Holding GmbH ("Landlord") and Nimbus Software GmbH ("Tenant")

1. Purpose

This Amendment implements the annual rent review under Section 4 of the Lease dated 1 March 2024.

2. Rent Adjustment

2.1 Based on the CPI change of +4.2% over the preceding twelve months, effective 1 January 2025 the base rent is increased from EUR 12,600.00 to EUR 13,129.20 per month.

2.2 The monthly service charge of EUR 1,890.00 remains unchanged.

3. No Other Changes

3.1 All other terms of the Lease remain in full force and effect, including the term, renewal option, parking, subletting and indexation provisions.
